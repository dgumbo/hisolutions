Few Words About Us

Heritage Innovative Solutions, established in 2015, is one of the uprising talented startups that aspires to be one of the leading technological companies that offers high quality products and services in the ICT Sector.

Heritage Innovative Solutions is a consulting-oriented company, intending to fill in the market need for a professional, customer-oriented computer company. The company emphasizes service and support to differentiate itself from more price-oriented computer companies. We are on the brink of penetrating a lucrative market in a rapidly growing industry. The current trend towards an increase in the number of entrepreneurs and competition amongst existing companies presents an opportunity for Heritage Innovative Solutions to penetrate this market.

Through monitoring of new trends, market demands and customer needs, we strive to offer the latest products and give the most relevant solutions for any business need.
